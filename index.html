Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents need to know before applying to Hillside Christian College for 2026.

What documents do I need to apply?+
You need 12 documents: the signed application form, signed admission policy (both parents + 2 witnesses), signed indemnity form (2 witnesses), account holder details, certified copy of learner's birth certificate, certified ID copies of both parents, certified copy of medical aid card, 2× passport photos, vaccination card (Grade 1 only), proof of EFT payment, a progress report from the current school, and a testimonial from your Pastor or church leader.
Is there a waiting list for the following year?+
No. Applications are only valid for the current academic year and no waiting list is maintained. You must submit a new application form each year, at least 2 months before the end of the school year, to renew enrolment for the following year.
What is the notice period if I withdraw my child?+
Three calendar months written notice, calculated from the 1st of a month, is required to withdraw a learner. Please note that November does not count as a notice month. Interest of 15% per annum is charged on arrear accounts.
Does my child need their own device for school?+
From Grade 3 upward, learners require a 10" tablet with keyboard or laptop. You may choose to provide your own device, or rent a school device for an additional N$550 per month. The school may block and remove non-curriculum content from any device used on the school network.
How do I pay school fees?+
Fees are payable monthly in advance on the 1st of each month via EFT or direct cash deposit to Standard Bank, Account No. 241510295 (Current Account), Walvis Bay. Use the learner's full name as reference. A N$100 penalty applies for cash payments. Email proof of payment to hillside.c.college@gmail.com.
What enrichment activities are available?+
We offer Chess & Robotics (N$150/month), Play-Ball sport (N$70/month), and optional After-Care homework classes Monday–Thursday 14:00–17:00 (N$450/month). All enrichments are available to all grade levels throughout the 12-month school year.
What happens if my application is incomplete?+
Applications will NOT be accepted if any of the 12 required documents are missing or if previous school fees have not been fully settled. Misleading or incorrect information will lead to immediate disqualification. All information on the form is mandatory.
